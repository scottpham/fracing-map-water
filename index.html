Waste Water Disposal Wells

There are tens of thousands of oil wells in Central California. Generaly they're hard to spot and blend in easily with the agricultural landscape. But several of these wells have been used by oil companies to dump toxic wastewater, including waste water from fracing operations.

The state admits that in nine wells, oil and gas companies were allowed to dump that water in protected aquifers that could have been used by people to drink or irrigrate.
